# World Intelligence Oil

## Vision

World Intelligence Oil is the first module of a larger intelligence platform designed to analyze global assets, geopolitics, trade flows, infrastructure, and world events.

The first focus is oil.

The system aims to connect:
- oil reserves
- oil production
- oil trade
- geopolitical events
- conflicts
- sanctions
- shipping disruptions
- market prices

to understand how global events influence commodity behavior over time.

---

## MVP Goal

Build an Oil Intelligence Dashboard that answers:

> Why did oil prices move, and which countries/events were involved?

---

## Initial Features

- Oil price timeline
- Country oil reserves and production
- Interactive world map
- Oil-related event timeline
- Country detail panel
